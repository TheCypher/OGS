Frequently Asked Questions

  • Provided below are brief answers to the most common questions that we would like to answer for you that are related to solar panels, savings, and how solar works.

  • Why decide to install solar?

    We don't need to convince you to spend money on electricity . Either way you will be spending money on electricity. However, you become your own power company, and this time you have control over the rates.

  • What is a fixed solar bill?

    If you qualify, there is no out of pocket cost or down payments. With current legislation, there is solar Incentive Tax Credit for residential system owners at 30% of the total system cost.

  • Is solar expensive?

    If you qualify, there is no out of pocket cost or down payments.

    With current legislation, there is solar Incentive Tax Credit for residential system owners at 30% of the total system cost.

  • What happens when I sell my home?

    You cannot simply unscrew the solar panels and mounting system, disconnect the solar inverter and take the whole system with you. The panels have to remain with your old home.

  • How does a home solar system work?

    A home solar system is made up of components that all work together to convert the power of sunlight into usable energy for your lights, appliances and devices.

  • Will I ever have to pay a power bill?

    No because your solar system will produce all the electricity for your home and you won't have to buy from the utility company.

  • What is installed as a part of a solar system?

    Each home is different, and the equipment we install can vary from house to house.
